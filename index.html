Welcome to the "Homelessness Support and Outreach" program, where our mission is to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those affected by homelessness and abandonment, both human and animal.

  • Our ultimate goal is to create a more compassionate and empathetic society, where abandonment and homelessness are not just statistics but human and animal experiences that we actively work to prevent and alleviate.
  • We actively engage with local communities, shelters, and organizations to raise awareness about abandonment issues and a collective commitment to making positive change, and through data-backed intervention strategies, we aim to prevent abandonment and homelessness by targeting the underlying causes, ensuring that individuals and animals remain in safe and caring environments.

Our "Homelessness Support and Outreach" program is more than just an initiative; it's a testament to our dedication to creating a world where no one is left behind.

Why Choose KARE.JAR.?

The project can raise awareness about homelessness and the challenges faced by seniors and vulnerable animals in the community. By providing information and resources, it can help educate the public about the issue.

Learn More

Data Analysis

Through data collection and analysis, the project can identify the root causes of abandonment and homelessness, enabling policymakers and organizations to make informed decisions and develop targeted solutions.

Support Services

The project can connect individuals and families experiencing homelessness, seniors in need, and vulnerable animals with essential support services such as shelters, food programs, medical care, and adoption services for animals.

Positive Impact

Ultimately, the project aims to create a positive impact on the community by addressing a pressing social issue and improving the lives of seniors and vulnerable animals.
